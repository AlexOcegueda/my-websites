Pizza

  • Time: 2 hrs
  • Serves: 12
  • Difficulty: Easy

Pizza is a popular dish which is quite easy to make. Today I will be presenting you a recipe so you can make your own homemade pizza.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon fine salf
  • 1/4 cup olive oil, divided

Directions
