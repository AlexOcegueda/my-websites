Salad

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves: 8
  • Difficulty: Easy

Today I will be presenting you the recipe for Cornucopia Salad. Cornucopia Salad is a great side dish that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 head red leaf lettuce, torn
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 Granny Smith apple, cored and chopped
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and chopped
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese

Directions

  1. Place almonds and 1 tablespoon of suger in skillet on medium heat. Stir until sugar melts and almonds are brown. Remove and let cool.
  2. Mix lettuce, onions, apple, avocado, cranberries, and blue cheese in a bowl.
  3. Whisk it all together with vinegar, 2 teaspoons of sugar and 1 teaspoon of salt. Vegetable oil is optional.

ENJOY!
